### [1.1.0] - 2019-08-12
#### Added
- Support for uploading screenshots to Slack!

Provide a `slackToken` (or env var of `SLACK_WEBTOKEN`), `slackChannels` and set `slackUpload` to true and watch your screenshots magically upload to Slack!
